Lasgana


Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese—typically mozzarella and Parmesan. Each layer is carefully stacked, then baked until golden and bubbling. The meat sauce often features ground beef or pork simmered with tomatoes, onions, garlic, and herbs. Vegetarian versions may use spinach, mushrooms, or zucchini. The result is a hearty, comforting meal with a perfect balance of savory flavors and creamy textures. Served hot, lasagna is a favorite at family gatherings and special occasions, celebrated for its delicious taste and satisfying layers.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
